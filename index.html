About Tala

Tala Prattoy upholds the art aspect of Durga Puja. Essence of creativity combining the tools and methods of art and installations interpreting the thematic stages of the festival. Tala Prattya's work process has always been on Durga Puja as a specific time-space where the public space is being transformed into an art venue. We are committed to make that art global.
